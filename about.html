Our Legacy

The Story Behind
DentCraft Excellence

Founded in 2015 by master technician Michael Chen, DentCraft began as a single mobile unit with a clear mission: deliver flawless paintless dent repair without the high cost and long wait times of traditional body shops.

Today, we operate a fleet of 28 mobile units across 12 states, having restored over 12,000 vehicles — from daily drivers to exotic supercars. Our gold-standard training program ensures every technician masters the art of light reflection and metal memory.

"We don't just repair metal; we restore the original line and beauty of your vehicle."

— Michael Chen, Founder
